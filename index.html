// About
How It Works
01
Reads Prediction Markets Jupiter API
Prediction markets are pools where people stake money on outcomes — for example, "Will SOL hit $200 this month?" The agent polls these every 15 minutes and tracks probability shifts, not absolute odds. A sudden jump in YES votes signals growing crowd conviction — far more meaningful than stable high odds.
02
Checks Token Health Jupiter Tokens + Price API
Cross-references crowd sentiment against on-chain data: organic trading volume (not bot-driven), real holder count, and whale concentration risk. This is the sanity filter. High sentiment + strong fundamentals = high confidence signal. Whale flag detected? Buy multiplier capped at 0.8x regardless of score.
03
AI Combines the Signals Groq llama3-8b (free)
A free AI model on Groq synthesises both inputs into a 0–100 score and recommends a DCA multiplier and OCO bracket percentages. Score above 70 = BULLISH. Below 30 = BEARISH, buys paused. The AI handles contextual weighting — a 10pp market shift means more when volume is also spiking.
04
Adjusts Your DCA Schedule Jupiter Recurring API
DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging) buys a fixed amount on a schedule. Market Whisperer makes it dynamic — a bullish signal buys 1.4–2x more, a bearish signal pauses buying completely. You automatically buy more when the crowd is confident and pull back when they are not.
05
Auto Brackets Every Buy Jupiter Trigger API
Every DCA buy automatically gets an OCO (One Cancels Other) bracket: a Take Profit order and a Stop Loss order. When one fills, the other cancels automatically. No manual monitoring needed. The TP/SL ratio is always asymmetric (minimum 1.3:1) so you let winners run further than losers cut.
06
Executes on Solana Jupiter Swap V2
All swaps route through Jupiter Swap V2 — the best price aggregator on Solana. It finds the cheapest path across all DEXes automatically. Gasless swaps are built in. The new unified /order + /execute endpoint handles landing and best price across all routers.
